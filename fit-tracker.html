How to Play

Welcome to the Fit Family Streak Challenge!

This is our family's fun way to encourage each other to build healthy habits together. The most important rule is that you win just by playing! The goal isn't to be the best, but to support each other.

How It Works: Three Daily Check-ins

Each day, pick your name and answer three quick questions. You can do all three, or just the ones that apply!

  • ⭐ Did you exercise? "Yes" earns a Gold Star (5 points). Add your workout type(s) and minutes — and you can even add a daily message for everyone to see.
  • 🥕 Did you eat healthy? "Yes" earns 2.5 points.
  • 💧 Did you drink enough water? "Yes" earns 2.5 points. (For anyone managing fluids, this question becomes "Did you stay within your fluid limit?" — so the healthy choice still earns the points.)
  • Save: Hit "Save My Entry" and you're done for the day! A perfect day is 10 points.

Seasons: Everyone Gets a Fresh Start

The challenge runs in 3-month seasons. At the start of each new season, everyone's score resets to zero — so if you've fallen behind, a clean slate is always around the corner. Your past entries stay saved in your calendar; they just don't carry into the new season's score. The current season's dates are shown under the title.

Streaks, Bonuses & Ranking

  • Streaks & Fire Emojis: String together consecutive exercise days and fire emojis appear next to your name — the longer the streak, the more flames!
    • 3 Days: 3d 🔥
    • 5 Days: 5d 🔥🔥
    • 10 Days: 10d 🔥🔥🔥
    • 20 Days: 20d 🔥🔥🔥🔥
    • 30+ Days: 30d 🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥
  • Streak Bonus Points: Earn a one-time bonus of 12.5 points the first time each season you reach a 5-day, 15-day, and 25-day streak. (Breaking and restarting won't re-earn it!)
  • Leaderboard Ranking: Your rank is based on total points this season. Ties are broken by total minutes exercised.

Your Personal Summary

  • Click Your Name: See a breakdown of your total minutes and all your activities.
  • Edit Past Entries: Click edit under your name to fix any day on your calendar.

Have fun, and let's get moving!
