How to Play

Welcome to the Summer Streak Challenge!

This is our family's fun way to encourage each other to move a little bit every day this summer. The most important rule is that you win just by playing! The goal isn't to be the best, but to support each other and build healthy habits together.

How It Works: The Basics

Every day, your goal is to do at least 1 minute of exercise.

  • Log Your Day: Go to the tracker webpage.
  • Select Your Name: Choose your name from the dropdown list.
  • Answer the Big Question: Did you get your exercise in?
    • Click "Yes" if you exercised for at least 1 minute. This gets you a Gold Star ‚≠ê!
    • Click "No" if you didn't manage to get it in. No worries, there's always tomorrow!
  • (Optional) Add Details: If you clicked "Yes," you can add more details about your workout(s), including the type of activity and how many minutes you did. You can even add a daily message for everyone to see!
  • Save: Hit "Save My Entry" and you're done for the day!

The Main Game: Streaks, Points & Ranking

  • Gold Stars & Points: Every day you earn a Gold Star ‚≠ê, you get 10 points. These points determine your main rank on the leaderboard.
